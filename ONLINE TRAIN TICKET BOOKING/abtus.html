Our Train History

At Online Train Ticket Booking, we take pride in our rich history and commitment to providing efficient train services to travelers. Our journey started over a century ago, and we have consistently evolved to meet the needs of our passengers.

Key milestones in our history include:

  • Established in 1905.
  • Introduced the first high-speed train service in 1960.
  • Expanded to international routes in 1985.
  • Embraced online ticket booking in 2005.
  • Continued innovations in safety and comfort.
