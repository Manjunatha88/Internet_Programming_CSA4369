About Us

Ko-matha Dairy Farm is a family-owned and operated business that has been in operation for over 20 years. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality dairy products and the best possible service.

Our cows are free-grazed on lush pastures and are fed a nutritious diet. We use state-of-the-art milking and processing equipment to ensure that our milk is clean, safe, and fresh.

We are also committed to sustainable farming practices. We use solar power to generate electricity for our farm and we recycle all of our waste. We are also working to reduce our carbon footprint by planting trees and using energy-efficient equipment.
